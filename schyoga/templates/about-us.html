I'm Kristyn - a Yoga Alliance Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT-200), specializing in therapeutic and prenatal yoga. I currently teach at The Fitness Haven in New Haven, CT and Eco Yoga in Durham, CT. My love of travel and desire to share yoga with those in need has led me to teach in Thailand, Cambodia, Uganda and, soon, Nicaragua.

So I travel and move a lot. My schedule is always in flux. I have two kids whose schedules throw additional fun into the mix. Yoga, however, is always a constant. It benefits me everyday: body and mind. Even as a teacher - especially as a teacher - I need to keep taking classes and learning. Sometimes, it's just hard to find a class to fit my schedule.

One weekend, I was headed to NYC to visit friends and wanted to check out one of the city's legion yoga studios. I said to my husband, "Wouldn't it be nice if there was a website that compiled all the yoga schedules for Manhattan?"

My husband Max, a software architect, replied "Yes. Let's do it." And that was that. He stared working late nights and weekends on www.ScheduleYoga.com, driven by my need, and the need of others, to find the perfect class at a time that works.

Currently, www.ScheduleYoga.com covers Manhattan but stay tuned for your city. This is not a site where studios sign up - Max has built a system that trolls for schedules and puts them in one place. This site will soon cover Seattle, San Francisco and Boston before going nationwide.
